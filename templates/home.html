How a prediction gets made

01

Enter monthly rainfall

You provide rainfall (mm) for each month — the same shape of data collected from weather stations.

02

Features are scaled

Inputs pass through the same StandardScaler fit during training, so the model sees numbers on familiar ground.

03

{{ model_name }} scores the risk

The classifier that performed best across Decision Tree, Random Forest, KNN and XGBoost comparisons makes the call.

04

You get a clear result

A flood-chance or no-flood-chance page, with a confidence score and practical next steps.
